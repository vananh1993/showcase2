HMO (2’-FL) is a prebiotic which has the same structure to nutrients found in breast milk. It promotes the growth of probiotics, prevents pathogen adhesion to the intestinal epithelial cell walls and support healthy digestion and immune system together with FOS..
Probiotics BB-12TM and LGGTM help increase beneficial bacteria and the ability to absorb essential nutrients.
Whey protein rich in Alpha-lactalbumin helps absorb nutrition easily.
Supplement 25% of DHA from algae (2), support brain development, which meets the DHA daily intake recommendation of the World Health Professionals FAO/WHO (3).

Rich in vitamin D and Calcium with Calcium and Phosphorus in the appropriate ratio (Ca:P), which is vital for bone health and height development.
